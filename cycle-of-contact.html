Why this matters in session

Most people don't get stuck at random — they tend to interrupt the cycle in a similar place each time. Some go numb before a sensation is even fully felt. Others get all the way to mobilization and then talk themselves out of acting. Others act, but can never quite let themselves feel satisfied.

Naming where your own pattern tends to break is often more useful than any amount of general insight — it gives us something concrete to work with together.
